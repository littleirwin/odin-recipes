Lasagna

An easy recipe for a very tasty lasagne.

Follow this recipe in order to make a simple yet tasty lasagna dish.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Bolognese sauce
  • Onion
  • Minced meat
  • Cheese
  1. Brown off the minced meat
  2. Add in the onions and cook until softened
  3. Add in the bolognese sauce and cook for 5 minutes
  4. In a baking dish, place a pasta sheet and cover with sauce. Repeat
  5. Top with cheese and bake for 45 minutes at 200 degrees
