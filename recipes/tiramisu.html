Tiramisu

An easy recipe for a very tasty tiramisu.

Follow this recipe in order to make a simple yet tasty tiramisu dessert.

Ingredients

  • Marscapone
  • Coffee
  • Chocolate powder
  • Bread Sticks
  1. Soak bread sticks in coffee
  2. Layer breadsticks in marscapone cream
  3. Sprinkle the top with chocolate powder
  4. Refrigerate for 3 hours
