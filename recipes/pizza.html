Pizza

An easy recipe for a very tasty pizza.

Follow this recipe in order to make a simple yet tasty pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Toppings of choice
  • Cheese
  1. Spread sauce over rolled out pizza dough
  2. Add toppings of choice
  3. Sprinkle with grated cheese
  4. Bake for 12 minutes at 240 degrees
